Sportscove – Mobile App

Sportscove is a mobile platform that connects aspiring athletes and sports learners with experienced coaches from around the world. It simplifies the process of discovering, booking, and interacting with sports mentors for skill development.

As the lead UX/UI designer, I handled everything from initial user flows and wireframes to high-fidelity mockups, prototyping, and developer handoff. I focused on making the onboarding intuitive and coaching interactions smooth and accessible.

Tools used: Figma, Illustrator, After Effects (for microinteractions), Notion (for documentation).
